## 🚀 What's New

### Advanced Analytics
Your app now uses **Expected Goals (xG)** and **Expected Assists (xA)** - the same metrics used by professional football analysts and Premier League teams!

### Key Features:
1. **xG (Expected Goals)**: Shows if a player is unlucky (high xG, low goals) or on a hot streak (low xG, many goals)
2. **xA (Expected Assists)**: Identifies creative players making quality chances
3. **ICT Index**: Overall influence, creativity, and threat combined
4. **AI Insights**: Smart reasoning for each prediction using all advanced metrics

## 📊 How to Use

### 1. Player Predictions
- View top predicted players
- Filter by position (GK/DEF/MID/FWD)
- See xG, xA, and ICT for each player
- Read AI reasoning for insights

### 2. Understanding the Metrics

**Yellow Badge (xG)**: How many goals they should score
- xG 5.0, Goals 3 = "Due 2 goals" ✅
- xG 2.0, Goals 5 = "Hot streak" ⚠️

**Blue Badge (xA)**: Quality of chances they create
- High xA = Great creative player
- xA > Assists = About to get more assists

**Green Badge (ICT)**: Overall impact
- 200+ = Elite player
- 150-200 = Excellent
- 100-150 = Good
- < 100 = Average

### 3. AI Insights
Look for these smart predictions:
- "Underperforming xG - due X goals"
- "Outperforming xG - hot streak"
- "Hidden gem: High xGI, low ownership"
- "Creating chances (xA: X) and delivering"

## 💡 Smart FPL Strategies

### Find Value Picks
**Look for:**
- High xG + Low ownership = Hidden gem
- High xA + Low price = Assist potential
- High ICT + Under 10% owned = Differential

### Avoid Traps
**Watch out for:**
- Low xG but many goals = Unsustainable luck
- High ownership but low xG = Overpriced
- Low ICT = Limited impact

### Transfer Timing
**Buy when:**
- xG >> Goals (regression coming)
- xA trending up
- ICT improving weekly

**Sell when:**
- Goals >> xG (luck running out)
- xA dropping
- ICT declining

## 📞 Support

### Common Questions:

**Q: What if predictions are wrong?**
A: xG is probability-based. A player with high xG should score "over time" - not necessarily next week. It's about trends!

**Q: Why use xG over goals scored?**
A: Past goals don't predict future - xG shows who's getting chances. More chances = more future goals.
